turn away verb
turned away; turning away; turns away

transitive verb

1
a

: to refuse admittance, acceptance, or access to

The bouncer turned away the people without tickets.

b

: to send away : reject, dismiss

The manager turned the applicant away after the interview.

c

: to push with a physical or emotional distance : repel

The smell of the trash turned him away.

2

: deflect, avert
