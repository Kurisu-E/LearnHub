Tunisia geographical name
Tu·​ni·​sia tü-ˈnē-zh(ē-)ə, tyü-, -ˈni-

country in northern Africa bordering on the Mediterranean Sea capital Tunis area 63,170 square miles (163,610 square kilometers), population 11,516,000

Note: Tunisia was formerly one of the Barbary States. It was a French protectorate from 1881 to 1956 and a monarchy in 1956-57. It has been a republic since 1957.
