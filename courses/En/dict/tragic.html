tragic adjective
trag·​ic ˈtra-jik
variants or less commonly tragical ˈtra-ji-kəl
1
a

: regrettably serious or unpleasant : deplorable, lamentable

a tragic mistake

The hikers made the tragic decision to continue despite the storm.

The tragic accident was caused by a moment of carelessness.

b

: marked by a sense of tragedy

the tragic loss of her parents

The news of her illness brought a tragic sense of despair to her family.

2

: of, marked by, or expressive of tragedy

the tragic reminder

the collapse of the Twin Towers was a tragic symbol

the tragic significance of the atomic bomb
— H. S. Truman
