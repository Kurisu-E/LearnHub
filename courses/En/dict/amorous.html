amorous adjective
am·​o·​rous ˈa-mə-rəs ˈam-rəs
1

: strongly moved by love and especially sexual love

amorous couples

He has an amorous nature.

She was feeling amorous.

2

: being in love : enamored → usually used with of

She was always amorous of her childhood friend, but he never noticed.

3
a

: indicative of love

received amorous glances from her partner

He sent her an amorous letter with sweet words of affection.

b

: of or relating to love

an amorous novel

The play had an amorous theme, revolving around a couple's forbidden love.
