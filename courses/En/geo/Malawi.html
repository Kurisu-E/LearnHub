Malawi geographical name
Ma·​la·​wi mə-ˈlä-wē
variants or formerly Nyasaland nī-ˈa-sə-ˌland, nē-

country in southeastern Africa bordering on Lake Malawi, but otherwise landlocked; formerly a British protectorate; an independent member of the Commonwealth of Nations since 1964; a republic since 1966; capital Lilongwe area 45,747 square miles (118,484 square kilometers), population 19,843,000
