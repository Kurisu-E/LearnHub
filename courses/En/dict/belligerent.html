belligerent adjective
bel·​lig·​er·​ent bə-ˈlij-rənt -ˈli-jə-
1

: inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness

a belligerent remark

belligerent speeches

belligerently adverb
2

always used before a noun : waging war

especially : belonging to or recognized as a state at war and protected by and subject to the laws of war

belligerent nations/states

belligerent noun
countable

An international group is trying to negotiate a cease-fire between the belligerents.
