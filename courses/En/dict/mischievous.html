mischievous adjective
mis·​chie·​vous ˈmis-chə-vəs ˈmish-; nonstandard mis-ˈchē-vē-əs mish-
1

: harmful, injurious

mischievous gossip

2

: tending to cause minor trouble (usually in a playful way): such as

a

: able or tending to cause annoyance, trouble, or minor injury

b

: irresponsibly playful

mischievous behavior

mischievously adverb
mischievousness noun
