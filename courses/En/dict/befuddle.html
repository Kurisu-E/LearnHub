befuddle transitive verb
be·​fud·​dle bi-ˈfə-dᵊl bē-
befuddled; befuddling; befuddles
1

: to muddle or stupefy with or as if with drink

... befuddled with drink all the time.
— Ellen Glasgow

2

: confuse, perplex

a problem that still befuddles the experts

befuddlement bi-ˈfə-dᵊl-mənt bē- noun

Word History

First Known Use

1801

Etymology

be- + fuddle
