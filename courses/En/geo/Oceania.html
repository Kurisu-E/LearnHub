Oceania geographical name
Oce·​a·​nia ˌō-shē-ˈa-nē-ə -ˈā-

the lands of the central and southern Pacific Ocean including Micronesia, Melanesia, and Polynesia (including New Zealand)

NOTE: Some people also consider Australia and the Malay Archipelago to be part of Oceania.

Oceanian ˌō-shē-ˈa-nē-ən -ˈā- adjective or noun
