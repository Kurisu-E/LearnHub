peafowl noun
pea·​fowl ˈpē-ˌfau̇(-ə)l

: any of three large terrestrial pheasants (Pavo cristatus and P. muticus of southeastern Asia and Afropavo congensis of central Africa) often raised as ornamental fowls due to their usually colorful, iridescent plumage and especially for the long, trailing tail converts of the male which can be held more or less erect and outspread

→ see also peacock, peahen
