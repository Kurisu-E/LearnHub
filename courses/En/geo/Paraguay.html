Paraguay geographic name
Par·​a·​guay ˈper-ə-ˌgwī, -ˌgwā, ˈpa-rə-; ˌpä-rä-ˈgwī
1

landlocked country in central South America traversed by the Paraguay River and bisected by the Tropic of Capricorn; a republic; capital Asunción area 157,048 square miles (406,752 square kilometers), population 7,026,000

2

river 1584 miles (2549 kilometers) long in central South America flowing from the Mato Grosso Plateau in Brazil south into the Paraná River in Paraguay
