have

1 of 2 verb
/hæv/ in "have to" meaning "must" usually /hæf/
had having has

Meaning"must" usually has

Transitive verb

  • 1a: to hold or maintain as a possession, privilege, or entitlement
    • they have a new car
    • I have my rights
  • 1b: to hold in one's use, service, regard, or at one's disposal
    • the group will have enough tickets for everyone
    • we don't have time to stay
  • 1c: to hold, include, or contain as a part or whole
    • the car has power brakes
    • April has 30 days
  • 2: to feel obligation in regard to → usually usedwith an infinitive with to
    • we have things to do
